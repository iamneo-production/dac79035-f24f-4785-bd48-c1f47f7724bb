HEAT WAVE PREDICTION IN TIER 2 CITIES OF TELANGNA -2023

The India Meteorological Department (IMD) declares a heatwave when the maximum temperature in a particular location reaches or exceeds a threshold value, which varies depending on the location. The threshold values for a heatwave are typically defined as temperatures that are significantly higher than the average maximum temperature for that location during the summer season.
